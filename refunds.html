Refund & Cancellation Policy

At The Outfits, we aim to provide you with the best shopping experience. If you are not entirely satisfied with your purchase, we’re here to help.

1. Cancellation Policy:
Orders can be cancelled within 12 hours of placing the order. Once shipped, cancellations are not accepted.

2. Refund Policy:
Refunds are processed only if the product is damaged, defective, or not as described. You must contact us within 2 days of delivery to be eligible for a refund.

3. Process:
To initiate a return or refund, please email outfits955@gmail.com with your order ID, issue details, and supporting images (if applicable).

4. Refund Timeline:
Approved refunds will be credited to your original payment method within 7–10 business days.

5. Non-refundable Items:
Accessories, discounted products, and custom-tailored outfits are not eligible for refund or return.
