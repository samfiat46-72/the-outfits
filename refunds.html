Refund & Cancellation Policy

At The Outfits, we aim to provide high-quality products and ensure customer satisfaction. If you are not entirely satisfied with your purchase, we’re here to help.

Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds are applicable only if:

  • The product received is damaged, defective, or incorrect.
  • A refund request is raised within 7 days of delivery.
  • The product is unused, unwashed, and in its original packaging.

Non-Refundable Items

Personalized, customized, or clearance sale items are not eligible for refunds unless damaged or defective.

Refund Process

Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you. If approved, your refund will be processed to your original payment method within 7–10 business days.

Order Cancellation

Orders can be canceled within 12 hours of placement, provided they haven’t been shipped. After shipment, cancellations are not accepted.
