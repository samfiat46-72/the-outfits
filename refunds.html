Refund and Cancellation Policy

At The Outfits, customer satisfaction is our highest priority. We take pride in delivering premium-quality products and strive to ensure that every customer has a seamless shopping experience.

Order Cancellation

Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placement, provided they have not yet been shipped. Once an order is dispatched, it cannot be cancelled.

Refunds

Refunds are applicable only for eligible cancellations or returned items that successfully pass our quality inspection. Once approved, refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 5–7 business days.

Non-Refundable Items

  • Customized or altered products
  • Items purchased on final sale or promotions
  • Products damaged due to misuse or wear and tear

How to Request a Refund or Cancellation

If you wish to cancel or request a refund, please contact us at outfits955@gmail.com with your order number and details. Our team will review your request and get back to you within 48 hours.

We reserve the right to amend this policy at any time to ensure transparency and compliance with business regulations.
