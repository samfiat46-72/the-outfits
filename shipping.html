Shipping Policy

At The Outfits, we strive to ensure that your orders are delivered in a timely and secure manner. All orders are processed within 1–3 business days after receiving payment confirmation.

Delivery Time

Once dispatched, deliveries within India typically take 5–7 business days. Delivery times may vary depending on your location and the courier partner’s network coverage.

Shipping Charges

Standard shipping is free on all orders above ₹999. A nominal delivery charge may apply to orders below this amount.

Order Tracking

You will receive an email or SMS with tracking details once your order has been shipped. You can use the tracking number to monitor your package through our courier partner’s website.

Delays and Exceptions

In rare cases, delivery may be delayed due to unforeseen circumstances such as weather conditions, public holidays, or logistics issues. We appreciate your patience in such situations.

Contact Us

For any shipping-related queries, please contact us at support@theoutfits.in
